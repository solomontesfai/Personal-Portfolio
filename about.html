About Me!

Hello! I am a current student at Clovis Community College indulging myself in the studies of engineering. I have always been fascinated with technology. I have passions about being involved in innovations that can ultimately change people's lives for the better. I believe pursuing a career in engineering and immersing myself in this field, will allow me to be involved in the future of technology.

To support this future, I have spent my last two years of high school participating in the CART program. CART is a separate school that houses several ROP courses in different fields of studies. At CART, I was able to take an engineering and web applications class. Simultaneously, I was taking AP Computer Science and Principles at my home school.

Engineering introduced me to 3D-modeling CAD software. Web Applications dove into the hidden field of UX. Here, I was able to learn fundamental objectives when creating products or material. I was also taught how to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a website or game. Computer Science Principles educated me on the relationship of society and coding.

I plan on continuing this study at community college. Although Electrical and Electronics engineering is what I am striving for, engineering has a variety of branches that I may find an interest in. I love how engineering is connected in such a way, and I plan on using the skills I learned to pursue a potential future in the science of engineering.

Thank you for your time and consideration. Please help yourself in exploring the other pages in my portfolio to learn more about my experiences, personality, and examples of work I’ve done in the past.
